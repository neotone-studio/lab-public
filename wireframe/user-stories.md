# User Stories

### Personas

**Handpan Player.** Comes from a culture of acoustic intimacy, slow craft, and communal playing. Protective of what the instrument means. Wants to know this deepens rather than dilutes that world.

**Electronic Musician.** Values system building and live performance. Moves quickly with high technical literacy. Wants to know what this adds that nothing else can.

**Music Lover.** A serious listener who wants to become a maker but hasn't found the right entry point. Has encountered technical barriers, gatekeeping, unfamiliar scenes. Wants to feel that this is genuinely for them.

---

### Design Principles

The site frames before it informs. 
Depth is available but not required. 
Editorial register is itself an argument. 
Visiting is the beginning of a relationship.

---

### User Stories

**Home**

- As a visitor, I want to understand what Neotone is before I see product specs.
- As a visitor who isn't ready to buy an instrument, I want to find and purchase smaller items connected to Neotone.
- As a visitor who isn't ready to commit to any purchase, I want a way to stay connected.
- 🐣 As a visitor considering a purchase, I want to see all product tiers together in the same place, so I can orient to where my preference lies in the overall product line.

**One / Treangle**

- As a buyer, I want help understanding which instrument is right for me, so I arrive at the product page already oriented.
- As a buyer, I want to choose between stock and built-to-order.
- As a buyer, I want to customize my instrument and see an image of it.
- 🐣 As a potential buyer, I want to save a custom instrument configuration without committing to a purchase.
- 🐣 As a potential buyer with a saved configuration, I want to ask specific questions to Neotone about it.
- 🐣 As a potential buyer, I want direct, person-to-person contact with Neotone at any point in my journey.
- As a buyer, I want to understand what I'm committing to before I commit. Cost, lead time, and cancellation / refund options.
- As a buyer, I want to know exactly what will happen after I order.
- 🐣 As a buyer (stock), I want to see actual photographs of the specific physical instrument I am purchasing.
- 🐣 As a buyer, I want to be notified when new stock becomes available.
- As a buyer, I want to encounter relevant Tonefield articles, so my understanding deepens without having to go looking.
- As a buyer, I want to see what pairs naturally with what I'm already buying.
- As a buyer, I want to add multiple items and purchase them together.
- 🐣 As a buyer, I want to apply a referral code at the instrument selection step. Note: referral codes apply to full-price stock and built-to-order instruments only, not to B-stock or discounted items.
- 🐣 As a buyer completing a purchase, I want to arrive at a rich post-purchase landing that tells me what happens next and connects me to the Neotone world.
- 🐣 As a buyer who has ordered, I want to receive proactive communications about the status of my instrument.
- 🐣 As a buyer who has ordered, I want my expectations set immediately: production timeline, software access, and what communications I will receive.

**NeOS**

- As a visitor or new owner, I want to understand what NeOS is, why it exists, and what it can do, so I am oriented to the instrument's intelligence layer whether I am considering a purchase or have just received one.
- As an owner, I want to find and download NeOS, so I can get into my instrument without hunting around.
- 🐣 As a new owner arriving from a post-purchase landing, I want a clear path into NeOS so that onboarding into the software feels like a continuation of the purchase experience, not a separate task.

**Tonefield**

- As a reader, I want past issues to feel like an archive rather than a feed.
- 🐣 As a reader, I want to search by subject keyword and find relevant articles from both Tonefield and the knowledge base.
- 🐣 As a reader, I want to search for a specific issue of Tonefield and see only the articles from that issue.

**Artists**

- As a player, I want to understand the path from customer to Artist.
- 🐣 As an Artist, I want my profile to showcase my name, location, a short biography, links to my work and platforms, and any availability for sessions or demos, so that visitors can understand my work and reach me directly.
- As an Artist, I want to access my referral links and see my commissions.
- 🐣 As a visitor, I want to search or filter Artists by location, so I can find someone available for an in-person demo or session near me.

**Community**

- 🐣 As a Player within the Neotone Community, I want to share a scale I have designed, with my name attached, to the public scale library.
- 🐣 As a visitor, I want to browse and hear community-shared scales on the website.

**Selection (Cart)**

- As a buyer, I want to review my selection and complete my purchase in one place, regardless of where on the site each item was added.

**Admin**

- As an admin, I want to manage instrument inventory.
- As an admin, I want to update current lead time on the product page.
- As an admin, I want every order to create customer and instrument records automatically.
- As an admin, I want to add and update Artist profiles.
- As an admin, I want to add and update smaller items for sale.
- As an admin, I want to track referral code usage per order so Artist commissions can be calculated.
- 🐣 As an admin, I want adding new stock to automatically trigger a notification email to everyone on the stock waitlist.
- 🐣 As an admin, I want to set which Tonefield articles are featured in which content stacks across the site.
- 🐣 As an admin, I want to manage email subscription types (Tonefield notifications, stock alerts, general newsletter) with granular unsubscribe options per type.
- 🐣 As an editor, I want to write and publish Tonefield articles by issue.
