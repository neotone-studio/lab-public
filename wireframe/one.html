What Happens Next

1

Confirmation

You'll receive an order confirmation and your serial number by email right away. Your place in the production sequence is secured.

2

Workshop Contact

Someone from the workshop will contact you to confirm your configuration and answer any questions before the build begins.

3

Build

Your instrument enters production. You'll receive updates as it progresses.

4

Delivery

Your instrument will arrive with documentation, warranty, and setup guidance.

Can I change my mind?

Yes, at any point. Before your instrument is built, during production, or within two weeks of delivery. Cancel or return for a full refund, no explanation required.
