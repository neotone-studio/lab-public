The Neotone One is a handcrafted electronic instrument built and assembled in the Budapest workshop. Its nine sensor pads, arranged around a central Ding in the geometry of the Hang, read pressure, velocity, and placement with a fidelity that acoustic surfaces cannot approach. The body is solid hardwood, chosen for grain and weight rather than resonance. Each instrument is built to order and carries its own serial number: a permanent record of its place in the workshop's history.

The wood does something that no specification can quite capture,it changes the relationship between player and instrument at the level of feeling.

Every Neotone One ordered directly from the workshop includes a six-year warranty, full configuration of wood species, and an ongoing relationship with the people who made it. The Scale Wizard comes preloaded with twenty-two scales, each a different harmonic world. More can be added through Alchemy, the instrument's configuration environment.

Questions.

Can I change my mind? +
Yes, at any point between placing your order and two weeks after your instrument arrives. Cancel during production or return after delivery for a full refund, no explanation required. The instrument is built for you; we want you to be certain.
What does the warranty cover? +
Six years direct from the workshop. The first three cover all defects including shipping both ways. The final three cover repair at cost of labour only. Ordering direct is the only way to access this warranty, retail orders carry a shorter coverage period.
How does an Artist referral code work? +
If a Neotone Artist referred you, entering their code at checkout applies a 5% discount to your order. The Artist receives 5% in return. You can find an Artist's code in their content, or by asking them directly.
How does VAT work? +
EU customers pay VAT at the rate of their country, calculated at checkout. EU businesses with a valid VAT number may enter it at payment to purchase exempt. Outside the EU, no VAT is charged, import duties vary by country and are the buyer's responsibility.
Why does it take 60 days? +
Each instrument is built individually by hand in Budapest. The 60-day lead time reflects both the care involved and the current demand for the instrument. You will receive a progress update at the midpoint.
Why order direct rather than from a retailer? +
Ordering direct gives you the full six-year warranty, a direct relationship with the workshop, and access to stock instruments when available. Retail partners are a useful point of contact in some regions but cannot offer the same terms.
