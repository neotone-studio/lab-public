Common questions
What is a Neotone Ambassador code?
If a Neotone Ambassador referred you, enter their partner code here. It applies a discount to your order and records the referral. You can find an ambassador's code in their content, or by asking them directly.
How does VAT work?
EU customers pay VAT at the rate of their country, calculated at checkout. EU businesses with a valid VAT number are exempt and may enter their number at the point of payment. Outside the EU, no VAT is charged by us — however, import duties and customs fees are the buyer's responsibility and vary by country.
Can I return the instrument?
Yes. Your purchase is fully refundable at any point before you receive the instrument. After receipt, you have a 2-week window to return it in its original condition for a full refund — no questions asked.
What does the 6-year warranty cover?
For the first 3 years, we cover all repair costs and return shipping. For years 4 to 6, repair work remains fully covered — you are responsible only for shipping the instrument to us. This extended warranty is available exclusively when ordering directly from Neotone. Retail partners offer a standard 3-year warranty only.
Why is there a lead time?
Each instrument is built individually by hand in our Budapest workshop. The current lead time of 60 days reflects both the care involved in production and the demand for the instrument. Your place in the production sequence is secured the moment your order is placed.
What is the serial number?
The serial number is the instrument's unique identity in the Neotone ecosystem. It is assigned at the moment of purchase and reflects the instrument's position in our production sequence — a permanent record of when and where in our history this particular object was made.
